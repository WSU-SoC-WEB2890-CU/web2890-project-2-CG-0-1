Who Are We?

PsyBari is a clinically focused company with the objective to evaluate a person's candidacy for weight loss surgery through our customized questionnaire.

The Questionnaire

Our questionnaire was specifically designed for weight loss surgery patients. As such, it's important to emphasize that the people who are taking our questionnaire MUST be morbidly obese since the results will be sent to specialized doctors in obesity and those doctors will get in contact with the patient in question in the hope of preparing that patient for surgery.
