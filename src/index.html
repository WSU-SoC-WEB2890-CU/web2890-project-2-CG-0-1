Who Are We?

PsyBari is a clinically focused company with the objective of providing an easy and secure access to doctors for people who are in need of a weight loss surgery.

The methodology that PsyBari uses is a questionnaire that was specifically designed to evaluate the candidacy of a patient for weight loss surgery if the patient in questioned presented characteristics that deemed an immediate weight loss surgey since PsyBari is in contact with doctor and medical facilities which specialize in weight loss surgery for people who are morbidly obese.

Our Questionnare

As stated above, Psybari is a clinically focused company which uses a specific questionnaire to evaluate the candidacy of a patient for weight loss surgery.

However, do beware that Psybari is not only in contact with doctors and medical facilities specialized in weight loss surgery but also that the questionnaire was specifically designed for individuals who are morbidly obese.
